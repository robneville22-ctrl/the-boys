# Jaegers 9U — Season Media Library

> Photos and videos from the 2026 spring season. Used for end-of-season collage, highlight reels, and memories.

## Folder Structure
```
media/
├── photos/          ← Game day photos, practice shots, team pics
├── videos/          ← Game clips, highlights, Plaud recordings (if saved)
└── README.md        ← You are here
```

## How to Use

### After Every Game / Practice
1. Drop photos into `photos/` — name them with the date: `2026-03-22-grayson-hr.jpg`
2. Drop video clips into `videos/` — name them: `2026-03-22-zach-triple.mp4`
3. Quick phone photos are fine — quality doesn't matter, memories do

### Naming Convention
```
YYYY-MM-DD-description.ext
```
Examples:
- `2026-03-22-team-photo.jpg`
- `2026-03-22-grayson-home-run.mp4`
- `2026-03-25-pregame-warmup.jpg`
- `2026-03-25-zach-pitching.mp4`

### What to Capture
- **Must-haves:** Team photo before each game, big plays (HRs, great catches, strikeouts)
- **Nice-to-haves:** Dugout energy, boys celebrating, funny moments, warm-ups
- **End of season:** Group photo, trophy/awards, team dinner

### Season Collage Plan
At the end of the season, pull the best photos and clips to create:
- Photo collage (print for each family)
- Highlight reel video (set to music)
- Individual player highlight clips (great gift for parents)

### Tips
- Ask parents to AirDrop or share photos after games
- GameChanger sometimes captures photos — check the app
- Plaud recordings live in `game-notes/` but video clips go here
- iPhone Live Photos → export as video for action shots
